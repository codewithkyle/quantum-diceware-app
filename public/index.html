Should I roll the dice myself or use number generator?
Both are great options. Just don't use passwords with numbers or words that you think up yourself. It's always more secure to use random values.
How are these random words "more secure"?
Most of the time it's easier to "socially reverse engineer" someone's password by learning what numbers they prefer or what words they commonly use. Creating passwords constructed using words generated by truly random methods means it's less likely a malicious actor can guess your password.
How is this password generator quantum?
The numbers used by this generator are generated in real-time at The Australian National University lab using lasers to measure the vacuum fluctuations. The technical details on how this is done can be found in their publications.
